a cornucopia of wonders

Movement of Li on top of CoO2 layer

Green - Li, Blue - Co, Red - O

The video above shows the band structure of LiCoO2 monolayer when Li is moved away from the CoO2 layer. One can see the Nearly free electron like bands forming more rigidly as the Li is seperated from the CoO2. This shows that the 2 dimensional electron gas like band is actually formed by Li-Li interaction in its elemental state.
